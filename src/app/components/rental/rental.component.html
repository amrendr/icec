Salient Features

  • Close to Shands and UF
  • Clean and modern amenities
  • Affordable rental rates
  • Ideal for conferences, seminars, social and cultural events

The auditorium occupies 10,000 square feet and can seat up to 400 persons. The spacious stage area lends itself to multiple uses and features a large drop-down projection screen, music system and stage lights, which are also available for renting.

No cooking is allowed anywhere inside the building. However, the food-warming area containing microwave ovens, refrigerator and ice coolers, provides abundant space for handling food and beverages brought to the building.

Other amenities available for rental include a prayer room, a game room, a TV room, and library room. These rooms on the second floor can accommodate up to 30 persons per room.

The building features an elevator and is fully accessible. Meetings and seminars can be organized in a cozy & quiet atmosphere.
