Categories of memberships

  • Donor Member (donation of $5,000 or more)
  • Founding Member (donation of $1,000 - $4,999)
  • Regular Member (payment of $100 annual dues)
  • Associate Member (students)
  • Honorary Member

Benefits of membership

  • Maintain a strong and vibrant Indian community in Gainesville
  • Voting and nomination rights for ICEC Board
  • Free or Discounted entrance/participation fee to all ICEC events
  • Invitation to members only events like Annual Picnic
  • Members can only participate at Republic/Independence day events
  • Free youth group membership
  • Voting and nomination rights in Youth Group office bearers election.
  • Discounted price for ICEC offered classes(Ex: SAT)
  • Discounted price for Vendor booths at IndiaFest
  • Preference to members at ICEC organized events for Vendor booths/Cultural programs....etc
